Michael Gandelman, MSc

Michael Gandelman is a researcher and Network Architect at NVIDIA. Formerly a researcher at the Technion Israel Institute of Technology, his work centers on networking for Supercomputers, Data Centers, and Al Custers. Past work also includes involvement in physics research on topics of particle physics, plasma, and semiconductors. Michael is also a regular speaker on topics of Jewish History, Halacha, Technology, and Science.
